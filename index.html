Places you must visit

pritrithvi cafe

I had visited in december 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance of the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black sign board that says "pritrithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonablly good menu at reasonablly good prices. The food is delicious the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared between two people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebritys on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid dissapointment. Host the famous "pritrithvi theatre fest" which you must attend if possible.

Review on these movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my friends and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss out on 40% of the fun. You should watch infinity war,black panther , and age of altron-man and the wasp before watching Avengers Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to the most of other marvel super hero movies. You will enjoy it alot. There is ,at the beginning of movie,a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series the movie shows how the Avengers assemble once again in order to reverse thanos's actions. The movie lengthy,it does run for three all worth while.
